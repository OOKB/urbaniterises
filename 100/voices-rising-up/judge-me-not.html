Tender Bricks

For D. Jennings, April 29, 2015

by Sean Morrissey

who wrote poetry too from bible verses
and put them in grocery store
shopping carts because they keep
the fruit fresh hard candy clicking
past convoys and char as we drove
and sang about Telling It on a Mountain,
who thanked me for the ride
and prayed we meet again there.

Sean Morrissey
