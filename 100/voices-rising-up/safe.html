Safe

by Sheila Gaskins

I can’t take it-
I don’t understand it.
How come every time in a group setting, or workshop on race relations/diversity
Not always but a lot times
Some white woman/person will put in the group agreements the term
SAFE SPACE- S-A-F-E >space. That implies that there are spaces that are unsafe.
Congolese women in the Congo are constantly looking for a SAFE Space.
Why do you get to feel SAFE?
Safe to me from WHITE FOLK
• Is the void of conflict. Conflict is the bases of all drama,Theatre 101.
You can’t have a GOOD JUICY STORY WITH OUT CONFLICT
The News - conflict. Reality Shows - Conflict. This poem right about now - CONFLICT!!!
SAFE.
Why do you get to feel safe? - We don’t -
Safe for white folk
• Is the absence of pressure or stress.
Without pressure tires are flat.
Without stress - Rope won’t tie things together.
SAFE.
Did those 2 police officers feel safe in the comfort of their patrol car on that tragic day in November?
Did Emmitt Till feel safe when his final choice was a fatal choice cause he decided to whistle.
Why do you get to feel safe - they ~ didn’t?
SAFE – SAFE – SAFE!
News flash: Last time I checked SAFE was not a feeling. It’s an adjective meaning free from harm or risk.
Did Travon Martin feel safe as he was walking home in a hoody minding his own business in a “good” neighborhood” from the corner store? With a pack of skittles and a Lipton ice tea still in his hands. Safe. When Jackie Robinson would hit that line drive and run from 1st - to 2nd - to 3rd base and sliiiide into home plate and the empire would say - “SAAAFFE” Is that what you wanna feel ~ then go to Camden Yards!
(You do realize Jackie stole those bases, right. (Not earned))
Why do you get to feel safe - WE CAN’T!!!
Safe is a privilege, a luxury.
Safe is a given - to some.
Why do you get to feel Safe?
Why do you get to feel?
Why, do you –
Get?
All I know is every time you don’t feel “safe” - someone ends up dead!
- (SAFE) - (arm gestures are in an empire stance - silent)

Sheila Gaskins

Sheila “Strawberry” Gaskins is a Baltimore native who is unapologetic about her love for the city or the love for her Godgiven talent, Art. Her credits include BET Comicview, The Wire, and Last Comic Standing. She is a “humanist” like Meryl Streep minus all those Oscars. Join Artpartheid and friend her on Facebook.
